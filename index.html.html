About Me

A Professional Built
for the Global Stage

I am the sole Account Executive for Cameroon at Abegum Company Ltd, an international insurance intermediary. I service a diverse portfolio of clients, insurers, brokers, partners, and reinsurers operating across African insurance markets — with a particular focus on the UK, European, USA, and Middle East markets seeking African coverage.

I handle the full insurance value chain: from client advisory and policy placement through to insurer and reinsurer liaison, cross-border compliance, and claims coordination. Managing over $1 million in annual premiums across 50–100 accounts, I bring deep technical knowledge of African insurance market dynamics to every engagement.

Beyond insurance, I coach business and corporate professionals to strengthen their workplace English — covering spoken communication, professional writing, presentations, and job interview preparation. I also deliver freelance business documents and creative writing for clients who need high-quality, publication-ready content.
